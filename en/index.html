Experience, competence and countless application areas

Mirow Systemtechnik GmbH is a company with experienced engineers from the aerospace industry, who have extensive knowledge in the field of state-of-the-art sensors, measurement and data processing technology. We are active in the fields of research, development and production, as well as in the distribution of medical, sensor, measurement and control technology and in the data processing area.

Directly on the research

Thanks to excellent contacts with medical and technical universities and other research institutions, we work at the cutting edge.
We cultivate our partnerships on an equal footing - based on a high level of technical expertise and equipped with great curiosity about how things can work even better to achieve the greatest possible customer satisfaction.

Our sensor solutions

We specialize in areal multi-sensor systems and sensor arrays made of piezoelectric as well as piezocapacitive films for the detection of surface and clamping forces in the application area of mechanical engineering, flow measurement, vibration measurement and medical technology.

Services

Our services include sensor, electronics and software development for user-specific applications as well as the implementation of standardized measurement technology projects, including consulting, planning, training and distribution of the required hardware and software.

Customer-specific development services

  • Development and manufacturing of sensor arrays made of piezoelectric foils as well as the corresponding amplifier systems
  • Development and production of matrix sensor arrays made of piezocapacitive foils as well as the active electronic sensing components
  • Measurement systems for flow analysis
  • Measurement systems for vibration analysis
  • Software for data acquisition and analysis
  • Integration and commissioning at the customer site
  • User support and training
  • Support in the implementation and evaluation of challenging measurement series
