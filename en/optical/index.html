Optical Sensors

Optical measurement methods allow non-contact, real-time detection of the properties and deformation of materials and objects. We have extensive experience in this area, ranging from shape recognition with high temporal resolution to medical applications (plethysmography).

Examples for the use of optical measurement methods by us are:

  • Measurement of the oxygen saturation of blood
  • Highly dynamic deformation measurement for process control in agriculture
  • Detection and counting processes for industrial applications
